Privacy Policy

What we collect, where it lives, and what we never do with it — in plain language, not lawyer-speak.

Last updated July 2026

1What we collect

Trade Lee is a chart-reading practice game. Here's the full list of what we collect:

  • Email address — only if you create an account (with a password, or a passwordless magic-link sign-in). Used to sign you in, to send account emails (verification, password reset), and, if you subscribe to Pro, to send your receipt and billing notices.
  • Gameplay stats — accuracy, streaks, drills answered, Trader Rating, Ticks balance, and similar in-game numbers, tied to your account if you're signed in.
  • Anonymous usage events — page views, drill completions, and which firm links you click, tagged with a randomly generated device ID (not your email or name) so we can tell how many people are using the site and which pages work.

We don't collect your real name, phone number, address, or any financial/trading account information — Trade Lee never touches real money or a real brokerage.

2Where it lives

All of the above is stored in Supabase, a hosted Postgres database provider. Account rows and anonymous event rows are kept in separate tables — the event log is not linked to your identity. We don't run our own servers holding this data; it lives with Supabase under their own security and infrastructure practices.

3What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We don't run third-party ad trackers, ad networks, or cross-site pixels (no Facebook Pixel, no Google Ads remarketing tags).
  • We don't share your email with the prop firms linked from /funded/ — clicking through to a firm's own site is between you and them, and their privacy policy takes over from there.
  • We do use standard analytics (page views, click events) to understand traffic — this data is aggregate and anonymous, described in section 1.

4Cookies & local storage

Trade Lee doesn't use tracking cookies. We do use your browser's localStorage to remember things like your streak, drill history, and preferences on this device — that data stays in your browser and is never sent anywhere unless you're signed in (in which case it syncs to your account in Supabase). If you're signed in, an auth session cookie keeps you logged in between visits — that's it.

5Requesting deletion

Want your account and everything tied to it deleted? Email us and we'll take care of it.

Email tradeleeapp@gmail.com from the address on your account with "Delete my data" in the subject line. We'll confirm once it's done.

6Age

Trade Lee is intended for users 13 and older, consistent with typical terms of service for services like this. If you're a parent or guardian and believe a child under 13 has created an account, email us at the address above and we'll remove it.

!Questions

This policy may be updated as the app grows — check back here for the current version. Anything unclear? Email tradeleeapp@gmail.com and we'll answer directly, no support ticket runaround.
