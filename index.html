Frequently Asked

How do I practice reading charts?

Reps against real data with immediate feedback — see a setup, make a call, watch what actually happened next. That loop is what Trade Lee's practice mode and daily drill are built around: real historical candles, a decision timer, and an honest score, not a simulation.

Why do most breakouts fail?

Most breakouts are fakeouts — price pokes past a level on thin participation, finds no real buyers or sellers behind it, and snaps back. The Academy's Breakouts & Fakeouts unit breaks down exactly what to look for.
