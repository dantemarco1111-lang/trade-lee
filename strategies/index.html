Three setups worth knowing

No secrets, no black boxes — just the setups, the entries, and the honest weaknesses of each.

1Opening Range Breakout (ORB)

The most studied intraday setup — a 2023 academic study (Zarattini & Aziz) backtested a 5-minute ORB on US equities and found significant edge on high-volume days.

The setup
  • →Mark the high/low of the first 15 minutes after 9:30 AM ET
  • →Enter on a 5-min candle CLOSE beyond the range — not a wick poke
  • →Stop at the opposite side of the range
  • →Target 1.5-2x your risk
  • →Only trade it 9:45-11:00 AM, when volume is real
The catch Most breakouts fail in chop — it wins by catching a few big trend days, so expect strings of small losses. Never move your stop.

2VWAP Pullback

Institutions benchmark their fills to VWAP, so trending stocks repeatedly bounce off it.

The setup
  • →Stock trending clearly above VWAP with rising volume
  • →Wait for a pullback that TOUCHES VWAP and prints a rejection candle — long lower wick or engulfing
  • →Enter on that candle's close
  • →Stop just below VWAP
  • →Ride toward the prior high
The catch Dead in sideways markets — if price is crossing VWAP repeatedly, there's no trend to pull back within. Stay out.

3Breakout + Volume Confirmation

A breakout is only as real as its volume — this is literally what the daily drill trains your eye to see.

The setup
  • →Consolidation of at least 45 minutes with a tight range
  • →Breakout candle closes beyond the range on 1.5-2x+ average volume → higher odds of follow-through
  • →Weak volume breakout = fade candidate back into the range
The catch Volume confirms, it doesn't guarantee — even confirmed breaks fail into major levels overhead (prior day high, big round numbers). Always know what's above you.
